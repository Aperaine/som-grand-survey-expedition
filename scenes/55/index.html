The Forest of Many Tree-Like Things...
That Are Not Actually Trees

The Forest of Many Tree-Like Things was born from experiments gone wrong—when devs tried to copy-paste things from an LLM.
Each attempt produced something that looked almost right, but never quite: unknown species, fake branches, bizarre layouts.
Over time, these half-formed trees spread across the land, patching themselves together in a hell of a forest.
Now it grows endlessly, a monument to trial, error, and autocomplete.

Would you like to help catalog these strange things before the history gets too messy?
